About Me

I started this food truck in the midst of the pandemic, hoping to spread food from my culture around Pittsburgh. I am open from 11:30am - 2:30 pm each day! In my food truck, the specialty is korn dogs with sausage and cheese, but I offer a variety of options including kimbob, chicken, and mandu as well!
